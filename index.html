Our Courses

1 hour Try Scuba $35 and 3 hour Discover Scuba $95
Experience scuba before enrolling in a course. Equipment included. Full fee on Discover Scuba can be credited toward the OW course above.

Open Water (OW) Group rate $300 (if you can find a better local price, let us attempt to match it)
Classes are conducted year around call to schedule your class.
Lifetime certification to dive up to 60 feet anywhere in the world. Open water classes normally consist of 5 sessions including 2 evening classroom sessions, 1 evening pool session, and 2 open water diving sessions over two weekend mornings. One week and weekend only versions of this course are available. If your schedule does not permit this sequence of sessions, we also offer private classes. You may also select elearning by clicking on the icon on the right. BCD vests, regulator sets, tanks, weights, and wetsuits are included for the entire course in the course cost. Students must provide, rent, or purchase scuba grade mask, snorkel, fins, boots. Many area shops require you to purchase a lot of equipment to get certified. This may include weights, weight belts, bags, masks, snorkles, fins, and boots. Some shops even make you to rent wet suits, Bouyancy Control Devices and regulator sets to complete the course. So when comparing our cost to other shops make sure you include any hidden costs.

Adv. Open Water (AOW) $300
Includes two tank deep dive offshore trip worth $200 by itself! Improve your skills under supervision and be certified to dive up to 100 feet. Requires OW certification. No classroom work is required for this course. The course consists of 5 dives over at least two days. The required 60 foot plus deep dive for the AOW course is now conducted from our dive boat in 90 to 100 feet deep 50-100 foot visibility water full of sealife off of a rig or wreck in the Gulf instead of the rusted metal pipe with limited visibility sunk into the bottom of Mammoth Lake.

Rescue Diver $250
1-2 classroom sessions, 2 weekend morning or afternoon sessions in open water Current CPR/EFR required. Respond to diving emergencies. Requires AOW. EFR / CPR - $75, $50 if taken with Rescue Diver

Technical diving and specialty courses including enriched Air (Nitrox) -Price Varies According to Course
We are now teaching the PADI TEC 40 technical diving course for dives requiring decompression stops and will soon be teaching TEC 45 and 50 for diving below 130 feet. Enriched air allows serious divers to extend their bottom times. This is a one evening classroom course. Other Specialty Courses - available on demand including self-reliant diver, sidemount, public safety, dry suit, deep, dpv, lionfish tracker.

Also, any time you enroll in a course with us, you also receive free refresher course tuition for life.
The free refresher tuition does not include equipment rental, pool, or lake fees.
